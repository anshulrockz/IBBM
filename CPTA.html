Certified Programme in Technical Analysis (CPTA)

PROGRAM OVERVIEW

Certificate Technical Analyst (CTA) is a technical analysis training program offered by IBBM (Institute of Bulls & Bears Management). The purpose of this course is to impart knowledge and skills for becoming a successful investor, trader, analyst or advisor in the market. This course is a professional & comprehensive training program in technical analysis for traders & investors.
In finance, technical analysis is a security analysis discipline for forecasting the direction of prices through the study of past market data, primarily price and volume. In essence, the observation of the market itself with a view to deducing its probable next move from its past and current behavior. It is the only tool for predicting or anticipating Stock, Commodity and currency Market Movement for Intra-Day & delivery trades.

ADVANCE TECHNICAL ANALYSIS

Time Frame Analysis: Charts can be seen in various time frames like 5 Min, 15 Min, 1 hour, daily, weekly or monthly. A Time frame analysis gives you a better view of the market trend. That is something that are not seen on EOD charts may be visible in weekly charts thus a better decision making
Multiple Moving Averages: Applying multiple moving averages at the same time inorder to provide you with clear bullish and bearish views and reversals.
Combining Indicators: Various indicators can be combined to get more accurate entry and exit points. You’ll learn how to create your own indicators using various other indicators.
